One Columbia

student project

Brief:

Website redesign project for One Columbia: Arts, Culture, and History. The site's primary audience consists of those seeking upcoming events in the city of Columbia, SC.

The goal of the redesign was to streamline the site's functionality by eliminating clutter and simplifying the navigation.
